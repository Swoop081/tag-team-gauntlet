# LEGACY Pro Wrestling v8.2.2 Build 1

## Match Psychology 2.1

- Every match now contains at least three player decisions.
- Fast and one-sided matches use 3 decisions; standard matches use 4; longer wars, comebacks and tag showcases use 5.
- Decision points are distributed reliably through the match and cannot collapse into fewer prompts.
- Positive and negative decisions create persistent Psychology Momentum.
- Psychology Momentum changes who controls the automatic exchanges between decisions.
- Remaining Psychology Momentum is converted into the final Decision Bonus.
- Three or more entirely positive decisions now create a strong late-match protection against an otherwise arbitrary narrow loss. Upsets remain possible.
- The match hub displays Decision X of Y while a choice is active.
- The post-match decision list now shows the momentum generated by each choice.

## Test focus

1. Confirm every match presents at least 3 decisions.
2. Confirm standard matches usually present 4 and longer matches present 5.
3. Compare the live hub after Success and Major Success outcomes; the player's control and performance should become visibly more favourable.
4. Complete matches with all-positive, mixed and negative decision sets and compare win rates.
5. Confirm the post-match breakdown includes all decisions and momentum values.
