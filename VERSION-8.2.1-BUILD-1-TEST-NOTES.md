# LEGACY Pro Wrestling v8.2.1 Build 1

## Included
- Restores the Match Psychology 2.0 result overlay after every choice.
- Displays Major Success, Success, Mixed Result, Failure or Major Failure.
- Shows Match Score, Control and Crowd deltas before the match resumes.
- Re-renders the main match hub with updated values immediately.
- Adds pulse feedback to score and meter displays while the result is shown.
- Keeps the next decision locked until the result sequence completes.
- Removes the remaining top, bottom, left and right Career page frame borders globally.
