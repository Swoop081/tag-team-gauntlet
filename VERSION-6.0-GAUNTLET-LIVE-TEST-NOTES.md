# Version 6.0 — Gauntlet Live Foundation

## Test flow
1. Open Gauntlet Live from the main menu.
2. Choose one of four founders: Jack Mercer, Mason Marks, El Rey del Cielo or Max Justice.
3. Complete the full Monday-through-Sunday week. Monday and Friday are regular match days.
4. Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday provide persistent choice events.
5. Beat a wrestler for the first time to recruit them into My Stable.
6. Use Manage Stable to change the active wrestler.
7. Saturday is preparation day. Sunday is a weekly review in Weeks 1–3, while Week 4 Sunday is the monthly Supercard. The career then continues into the next month.
8. Close and reopen the game to verify the career persists through localStorage.

## Foundation scope
- Persistent weekly calendar and endless month progression.
- Four-founder restricted start.
- Stable collection/recruitment loop.
- Predominantly Singles matches with occasional Tag Team matches once the stable has at least two members.
- Persistent momentum, morale, recovery, next-match bonus and record.
- Triple Threat matches are reserved for the next match-engine expansion because the current engine is built for two sides.
