# LEGACY Pro Wrestling 8.3.7 Build 3 — Test Notes

This build consolidates the Week 1 and Week 2 QA pass.

## Implemented
- First-show commentary and news no longer reference events that have not happened.
- Show intros use evolving week-aware commentary and keep Start the Show beneath the show logo.
- Public challenges are stored and paid off with an interactive segment before the next match.
- Ethan Brooks is restored to ring-announcer duties; Katie Morgan handles ranking interviews.
- Legacy one-stat NPC choices now lead to consequence screens showing before/after values.
- Medical events identify the injury, severity, recovery time and risk before the player chooses.
- Career hub displays a persistent attribute-point reminder and explains where points can be spent later.
- My Career record synchronises with the active Career record.
- Level-up screens offer Spend Attribute Points or Later and explain the navigation path.
- Achievements are removed from the bottom of match reports and shown as dedicated celebration screens.
- Continue Broadcast appears at the top of the detailed match report; the long analysis remains available below.
- World Recap and Dirt Sheet include a top Continue to Next Day button.
- Winner screens remove legacy red side styling and artwork frames.
- Tag-team winner artwork alignment is tightened and tag victory copy acknowledges both partners.
- CPU Decision Impact receives a stronger participation floor.
- Victor Royale decision labels no longer use awkward mechanical Royal prefixes.
- Olivia Chase event copy and consequences are more specific.
- Build/cache identifiers updated to Build 3.

## Primary testing focus
- New Career Week 1 opening commentary and Tonight on LPW content.
- Public challenge interruption and return to the scheduled match.
- All NPC outcome screens, especially Leon Ward, Ava Cross, Dr. Lena Hart and Coach Hank Dawson.
- Level-up/attribute-point reminder flow.
- Singles and tag-team winner/result screens on iPhone-sized displays.
- CPU Decision Impact across several matches and wrestler types.
